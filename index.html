About

Every year Macoupin County government budgets and spends more than $7 million. The services the County provides with that money effect each citizen, but far too often citizens and taxpayers are left asking ‘How exactly is my money being spent?’

This budget transparency tool was created to answer that question. It gives visitors the ability to break down the County’s budget from the comfort of their living room. Nearly 20 years are included and broken down to individual line items like salaries and benefits, supplies and travel. Visitors can also use the ‘Who Controls It?’ feature to see how each Elected Official or Department Heads’ overall budget has changed over time.

Partial 2014 data

You’ll notice that no expenses are listed for Fiscal Year 2014. The data from this site was pulled directly from the county audits at the end of the fiscal year. The 2014 data will not be updated until that fiscal year is completed and that audit finished.
